Snowy Town - Platformer Tileset

This pack is suitable for adventure games, side scrolling games, RPG games and more.

-49 items and objects

-2 animated items and objects (coin, chest)

-1 layered background (3 layers) horizontally tileable

-3 different sizes (tiles of 256x256 px, 128x128 px and 64x64)
